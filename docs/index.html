About

English-Australian on paper but Spanish at heart, London-based travel journalist Isabella has been wandering the globe since her first round-the-world trip as a one-year-old. Having grown up in a whitewashed Andalucian village (and picking up a lifelong malagueño accent), she is a Spain specialist, but also writes extensively about India, Southeast Asia, Greece, the UK and beyond.

She writes for Lonely Planet, Telegraph Travel, Etihad Airways’s Atlas magazine and various other print and digital media. Isabella also works as a sub-editor, is a Spain expert for Telegraph Travel and has contributed to over 30 Lonely Planet guides, from Pocket Ibiza and Pocket Phuket to multiple editions of India, Spain and Andalucía.
